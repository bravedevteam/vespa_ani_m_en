King's Raid:
Successors of the Will

Created by the Goddess of Light <Lua>, the Orbis Continent accounts for more than half of the world, and it is the place where humans and various races live. The story takes place in the <Kingdom of Orvelia>, a country that produced King Kyle who bravely fought in the war against the invasion from the Dark Lord that came from the Pandemonium 100 years ago, <Angmund>.

Kyle and his comrades from other races stood
against the Dark Legion and defeated Angmund
at last, but Kyle too disappears from the battlefield.

A squire named Kasel was living in the Kingdom of Orvelia that was enjoying the period of peace ever since King Kyle had defeated the Dark Lord Angmund 100 years ago. However, his fate begins to turn as he hears the news that Demons have appeared in the vicinity. At the request of the Great Sage, Kasel and his dependable comrades set off on a journey to find the sealed Holy Sword.

Episode 1
The Demons that Lurk in the Moonlight
It's been 100 years since the Dark Lord Angmund has fallen by the king of Orvelia, Kyle. Kasel, a squire of the Guardian Knights, has been receiving his daily training by his senior Clause to become a knight that can protect people dear to him. Meanwhile, there is news that Demons that should've disappeared from the King's Forest of Orvelia have appeared again. As Clause and his troops are being dispatched to the forest to inspect the Demons, Kasel wishes him luck and awaits his return.
Episode 2
The Truth, Revealed
In order to save his comrades, Kasel proceeds to the King's Forest with his childhood friend and a priestess, Frey. However, what they saw in the forest was the corpse of his comrades and Clause's shield. While they were driven to despair, a group of Demons suddenly approaches them. The Black Edge, the mercenary hired by Orvelia saves them, but Kasel becomes frustrated with his helplessness as a squire. Then, a wizard who calls herself Cleo and her escort, Roi appear and claim that they came to take Kasel and Frey to a certain place.
Episode 3
What the Predecessors Left Behind
Led by Cleo and Roi, Kasel and Frey arrive at the [Tower of Sage], a gigantic island floating between the clouds. It was Dominix the Great Sage that wanted them here. And Kasel hears the momentous fact from Dominix - 100 years ago, King Kyle of Orvelia formed an alliance with Elfs and Orcs to exterminate the Demons that were invading and attacking humans. Then, Kasel finds out that King Kyle confronted the leader of the Demons, the Dark Lord Angmund with the Holy Sword bestowed by the Goddess of Light, Lua.
Episode 4
The Forest and the Elves
After discovering his origin, Kasel desires to get the Holy Sword. Then, Kasel, Frey, Cleo, and Roi arrive at Elidora's Forest to find the first key that can unleash the Holy Sword, Aea, and meet the Great Sage of Elidora's Forest, Lorraine. Then, an Elf named Selene appears and tells them that she would guide them to Lorraine. However, Kasel and his party lose Selene on their way. Elidora's Forest, alias [Forest of Delusion], is famous for Elfs and Treants misbehaving to delude the travelers. That was when a small Treant appeared before the bewildered Kasel and his party.
Episode 5
The Power of the Holy Sword
The Great Sage Lorraine appears in front of Kasel and his party that came to Elidora's Forest to acquire the first key that unleashes the Holy Sword, Aea. However, instead of unleashing the Holy Sword, Lorraine called Selene and asked if [Kasel is worthy of acquiring the power of the Holy Sword, Aea]. What would be Selene's answer after she watched Kasel going berserk when a Treant was slain by a Demon?
Episode 6
The Maven of Fire
After unleashing the Holy Sword for the first time, Kasel and his party have been traveling to the Ogria Mountain to meet Aeatola, who holds the second key that unleashes the Holy Sword. Meanwhile, they decide to visit the tourist city called Holtshuten for a rest. Cleo becomes exhilarated as this was one of the cities that Cleo wanted to go after reading about it in the past.
Episode 7
The Destination of Hatred
The Black Edge is the mercenary hired by the Kingdom of Orvelia that makes a contribution by suppressing the Demons with El Moriham. Riheet was recruited by the supreme commander of the Orvelian Knights to [fight along as part of the Knights], but their real purpose was to take over Orvelia. Although the Black Edge was acting like an ally and gradually earning the trust of townspeople through the suppression of the Demons, some weren't pleased.
Episode 8
The Memory of the Eye
A girl appears before Kasel and his party as they were heading towards to the Ogria Mountain. Frey senses something from this girl who came from a refuge where the people that fled from the town that Demons have swept were gathered. Frey persuades everyone to spend the night at the refuge. Why did Frey make a sudden request..? And again, the great darkness that spreads through the world awakens.

CHARACTERS

  • Major
  • The Guardian Knights of Orvel
  • Black Edge
  • Others

Kasel

CVKaito Ishikawa (石川　界人)
A squire of the Kingdom of Orvelia. He is training every day to become a strong knight that can protect everyone. He spent his childhood with Frey and Clause at the same facility, and he thinks of them as his family. After finding out that Clause hasn't returned after he had joined the expedition, Kasel decides to go on a journey to save Clause.

Frey

CVAi Kakuma (加隈　亜衣)
A priestess who serves Lua, the Goddess of Light. She's also from the same facility where Clause and Kasel came from. She considered the path of becoming a priestess as fate, and her kindheartedness reflects her determination as a priestess. She joins Kasel's journey as she was worried about him.

Cleo

CVAri Ozawa (小澤　亜李)
A wizard of the Tower of Sage. She was raised by Dominix the Great Sage. Although she is a genius wizard, she is a troublemaker since she has never lived outside of the Tower of Sage. At the command of Dominix the Great Sage, she joins Kasel's party. She is excited about the outside world she could only see in books.

Roi

CVKengo Kawanishi (河西　健吾)
An escort of Cleo at the request of Dominix the Great Sage. Although he's a former member of a group of assassins, he escaped from it by risking his life for some unknown reason, and he hides his past. He's cold-hearted, but he's also an attentive and considerate person.

Scarlet

CVMitsuki Nakae (中恵 光城)
The only daughter of the current king of Orvelia and the supreme commander of the Knights. Even though she is a princess, she's courageous to go into the battlefield and has outstanding sword skills. On behalf of the current king who is ill, she is displaying the quality as an excellent leader by striving to protect the people including the country, and appointing people regardless of their origin and status.

Demia

CVMika Kanda (神田 みか)
A female knight of noble birth who leads the Guardian Knights of Orvelia. As a mighty and dignified knight who wields a heavy lance and a giant shield, she has pride as the commander and she's determined to perform any duties for the Kingdom of Orvelia and Princess Scarlet.

Clause

CVTakuma Terashima (寺島 拓篤)
The upright and righteous man who protects Orvelia as a knight. As one of the Guardian Knights, he's widely acclaimed with his sense of justice and skills. As he grew up with Kasel and Frey at the same facility, he considers them as his family. After hearing the news that Demons that have vanished 100 years ago appeared again at the King's Forest of Orvelia, he joins the troops to go on an expedition.

Riheet

CVRyota Suzuki (鈴木 凌汰)
A man who's considered as the leader of Black Edge that's consisted of strong Dark Elves. He's capable of fighting against hundreds of enemies, and he's providing support to Orvelia with a member of Black Edge as a mercenary. However, he is seeking revenge with strong hatred toward humans of Orvelia that harassed Dark Elves and killed his parents deep inside.

Ripine

CVYoshino Nanjo (南條 愛乃)
A sister of Riheet and a member of Black Edge. She promised revenge with her brother Riheet since their parents were murdered by the Orvelian soldiers. She prioritizes helping her brother over her own happiness. Of all the Black Edge members, she has the strongest hatred toward Orvelia, and she wishes to reduce humans that abused Dark Elves to obedience.

Tom·Azar·Zukka

CVTom : Gen Sato (佐藤 元)
Azar : Taishi Murata (村田 太志)
Zukka : Hinata Tadokoro (田所 陽向)
The Dark Elf trio that belongs in Black Edge with Riheet and Ripine. Azar is emotional, but he's friendly and the life of the party. Although he's quiet, Zukka saves everyone with his decisiveness. Tom is a lively boy. These three also have suffered from the persecution of Dark Elves. They're trying to change the world with Riheet.

El Moriham

CVShunsuke Sakuya (咲野 俊介)
A noble of Orvelia. He was born from a reputable family that constantly produced prime ministers. Although his family is currently falling, he's acting secretly to restore the former glory. He's an ambitious man who would do anything for his goal. In order to raise his status within Orvelia, he plans to forge an alliance with the Dark Elves, which was considered taboo. He maintains a cooperative relationship with Riheet, the leader of the Dark Elf mercenaries [Black Edge].

Maria

CVSakura Nakamura (中村 桜)
A mysterious witch who appeared along with the Demons. She seems to detest Lua as she cynically says [the Holy Sword is Lua's curse]. What could it be that she's hiding..?

Malduk

CVKentaro Ito(伊藤 健太郎)
A wizard that awakened from a hundred years of sleep to resurrect the Dark Lord Angmund. He's looking for the Holy Sword, Aea. Although he is a human, he's with the Demons to destroy everything in this world.

Dominix

CVRyotaro Okiayu (置鮎 龍太郎)
The Great Sage of Orvelia and the owner of the Tower of Sage. He's retaining his youth for over 100 years with the power of magic. He participated in the war against the Dark Lord Angmud 100 years ago since he had a great connection with the king of Orvelia, Kyle. He claims that maintaining world peace is his role.

Pavel

CV Yuki Inoue (井上 雄貴 )
A curious case of a human wizard possessed by the soul of an ice dragon. Having fought alongside Kyle 100 years ago against the Dark Lord Angmund, this ancient ice wizard has actually existed far before even then. Although he lived away from the concerns of the world until he met King Kyle, the ties with the world faintly continue as the disaster caused by what happened 100 years ago has completely changed his surroundings.

Kaulah

CVShinobu Matsumoto(松本 忍)
A powerful Orc shaman who wields the power of lightning. He is a respected elder of the Orc Alliance. He is also one of the few shamans who has clairvoyant dreams, and he is wise enough to properly interpret them.

Theo

CVShun Horie (堀江 瞬)
A knight who served the Republic of Gray 100 years ago, and a knight who swore his loyalty to the princess Jane Gray. Something significant happened to them 100 years ago.

Kyle

CV Masaya Matsukaze (松風 雅也)
A clever and courageous king that ruled Orvelia 100 years ago. Resembling the free-spirited previous king, he lived freely in his youth. He was succeeded to the throne at the period of despair when the Dark Lord Angmud was crushing the continent. As a king, he opposed the Demons by forming an alliance with all the races within Orvelia. At last, King Kyle confronted the Dark Lord Angmund and managed to stab his heart with the Holy Sword, Aea. However, both King Kyle and Angmund disappeared, and only the Holy Sword remained on the battlefield.

Lorraine

CV Emi Hirayama (平山 笑美 )
The Great Sage of Elidora and the wisest of the Druids. As one of the heroes who fought against the Demons with King Kyle 100 years ago, she is often considered the wisest person on the entire Orbis continent. The king of Elidora and the descendants continue to pay their regards to this day. Having recently sensed a strange disturbance from the Elidora Forest, she is preparing for the advent of the successor of the Holy Sword.

Reina

CVYuuki Takada (高田 憂希)
A mercenary of noble birth with an energetic and free spirit personality. Since shehe prefers a life of freedom as a mercenary over the suffocating life of a noble, it seems that she's completely lost all traces of her noble upbringing in her. Currently, she's wandering around the Orvelia after leaving the Empire.

Selene

CV Juri Nagatsuma (長妻 樹里)
This noble Elven princess admires the Holy Sword and its wielder. Since she is conservative as others of the forest, she's hostile towards things that are unrelated to the races of the forest. She loves all races that belong in the forest and tries to protect them from Demons.

Jane

CV Moe Toyota (豊田 萌絵)
The princess of the Republic of Gray that was located in Galluah Plains 100 years ago. Her relationship with Theo and what happened in the past remain a mystery.
